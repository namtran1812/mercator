# Mercator

Mercator is a distributed fixed-income pricing, temporal reference-data, and agentic market-intelligence platform.

## Core goals

- Support 10,000+ synthetic corporate bonds and fixed-income ETFs
- Process real-time quotes, trades, benchmark curves, and reference-data updates
- Perform incremental dependency-graph repricing
- Maintain bi-temporal instrument and identifier history
- Detect stale, conflicting, and anomalous market data
- Ingest attributed public market research
- Provide grounded LLM-assisted research and trade-analysis workflows
- Benchmark end-to-end pricing latency and information-retrieval performance

## Mercator vs. MiniMatch

MiniMatch models exchange execution and order matching.

Mercator models pricing, reference data, market intelligence, reliability, and research workflows. It does not contain an order book or matching engine.
